Personas

Jose

Jose is a 45 year old man. He is a father and currently works full time about an hour away from home. He gets up early to make sure he doesn't get stuck in traffic on his way to work each day. He works about an hour and half away from his home and doesn't have the chance to go back until his work day is over. When he comes home he looks for activities that he can spend time with his family. He likes being out in town, and always checks the internet to see if the local activities are appropriate for his family of 2 younger kids and a teenager.

Maria

Maria is a 20 year old who lives downtown and works in the same town. She is a college student and works part time at a restaurant 30 minutes away from home. She has to take public transport each day and hates being out in the rain for too long. She also exercises about three times a week and is always looking for new places she can go for a run. When she has free time she like to go activities that allow her to spend time with her friends.

Scenarios

  1. Jose woke up late for work on a rainy day. He needs to know if he can make it to work driving or if he should take the train.
  2. Jose wants to surprise his family by taking them camping on the weekend and wants to make sure what the weather will be like that weekend.
  3. Maria just got off of work and her friends as her if she knows of any activities they can go to. She wants to go online to quickly know what activities she could invite her friends to.
  4. Maria heard there's a charity run nearby and wants to know where it'll be.
  5. Maria and Jose both want to know if they need to take a jacket to work today. They both want to check the temperature for that day and will use their phone to do so.
