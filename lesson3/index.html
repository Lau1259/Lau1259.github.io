Weather Cues

Site Name

The name of my site is weather cues. I chose this name because I wanted something that sounded like you would get information quickly and thought of cue cards.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to quickly present weather forecasts to the target audience with suggestions for activities that fit the forecast. It should also display relevant activities within the region. It will also give traffic ratings for anyone in the area that indicate how much time people should plan to add to their commute based on the weather for the day.

Domain

I chose weathercues.com for my website's domain and it is available for $12/year. Google

Logo

I created this logo focusing on a sun with a cloud. The visual elements chosen are easily recognizable and relate to weather services.
